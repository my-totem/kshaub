Frequently asked questions

How long will it take?

The vast majority of papers take less than 24 hours. If you need it sooner let us know after uploading your essay and we’ll take care of it. An additional charge may be required for expedited delivery. Check our coupons for discounts.

Which payments do you accept?

We accept online payments through Visa, MasterCard, and American Express. If you don't have a credit card, we accept checks, PayPal (you must have a PayPal account), and wire transfers. To arrange offline payments, or if you have any questions, please contact our sales team.

Is there a long-term contract?

No, we don't force you into any long-term contracts. Our best price is available as an annual subscription, but we offer monthly subscriptions as well.

Do you have a free trial?

We sure do!  You may test Totem for as long as you need without entering your credit card.

How do transaction fees work?

During this time we found that our smallest customers accounted for a significant amount of our support resources. We have changed our pricing structure so that we can better meet the needs of all our customers.

How do I get the money I earn through Totem?

A Stripe account is required to accept payments in Totem and receive payouts. Money you earn is transferred directly to your bank account.

Can I share an account with a friend?

You cannot share accounts, but we do make it easy to invite friends and receive discounts. When friends signs up you both receive a 10% discount toward any plan. Get an entire year of formatting for free when 10 friends sign up! Request a link.

Do you offer any discounts?

We sure do! We have several coupons available right now. Click here to view our current discounts.
